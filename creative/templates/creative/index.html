Thuu Real

Ouuuuuuu—welcome to my YouTube channel!

Owning this space has been a dream for the longest time. I don’t see it as just “content creation”—it’s more like finally giving my big personality a stage to breathe, laugh, and connect.

Here, chaos is the niche. Talking too much? Definitely. Random adventures? Absolutely. But underneath it all, it’s about being unapologetically myself and inviting you to do the same.

Why Sincerely Thuu?

My life feels worth leaving digital traces—like a diary that always ends with a signature: Sincerely, Thuu.

At first, the name felt too elegant, almost like a perfume brand. But then I realized it captures exactly what I stand for. My chaos is authentic. My voice is pure. And everything I share is unapologetically me.

That’s why I can’t box my content into categories like beauty, podcasts, or travel. Y'all will just have to take whatever, however it comes even if is cooking. It’s not about fitting in—it’s about being real. It’s very me. Very Thuu.

About the Author

I’ve always loved writing. Back in the day, I used to fill notebooks with stories, and my friends would read them—it was so fun. I haven’t settled on one category yet, and honestly, I’m open to learning as I go. But my first novel-in-progress leans toward thriller: murder, a psychopath, and all the suspense. It’s nowhere near done, so I won’t say much… except y’all better wait for it (and don’t judge me!).

On November 6th, I published a set of poems via digital drafts. They share pieces of my experience as child number one. Being a firstborn is beautiful, but sometimes people—even my friends—forget that I’m a child too. I thought that was worth sharing.

I don’t overthink poetry; I just write. I’ve got plenty of pieces tucked away, maybe one day I’ll throw them into a book and call it Different Feelins (just kidding… or maybe not). My style is hybrid: not much rhyming, but full of sarcasm, metaphors, and truth. Honestly, some poems are better heard than read, so you’ll probably catch me performing them on TikTok and Instagram. So you better be following me, okay?
